# Drop Lists

RuneLite plugin for managing toggleable item lists that swap inventory left-click to **Drop**.

## Usage

1. Open the **Drop Lists** side panel.
<img width="89" height="62" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-14 at 11 40 30 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/930bd4de-416b-4e2a-a7ac-070f9ae5725f" />

3. Create a list and select it.
   
<img width="252" height="252" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-14 at 11 42 02 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dc9f326e-0ba9-4a94-8875-93484e00d06e" />

4. Add items by typing the exact item name and pressing Enter/Add, or by right-clicking inventory items and choosing **Add to drop list**.
<img width="242" height="262" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-14 at 11 42 22 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ab28df65-c83c-46b9-99cd-1b3e97226b97" />

<img width="316" height="144" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-14 at 11 42 58 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e6a7a905-99e4-4c17-b952-64e0f444b6bc" />
<img width="383" height="148" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-14 at 11 43 11 AM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b14259eb-4553-40a8-8ee0-37b842df1ed9" />
  
5. Toggle a list on to make left-click Drop for its items and toggle it off to turn off left-click drop.


Right click to delete or copy and share the JSON. Use import to create a list directly from the clipboard.
